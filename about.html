Our Story

About La Maison

Since 2010

A Legacy of Culinary Excellence

La Maison was born from a simple vision: to create a dining destination where exceptional cuisine meets genuine hospitality. Founded by Chef Jean-Pierre Dubois, our restaurant has become a beloved institution in the heart of the city.

What started as a small bistro has evolved into a culinary landmark, yet we've never lost sight of our founding principles. Every dish that leaves our kitchen carries the same passion and attention to detail that defined our first service.

We source the finest seasonal ingredients from local farms and trusted suppliers, transforming them into dishes that honor tradition while embracing innovation.

Our Approach

From Farm to Table

We believe that extraordinary dishes begin with extraordinary ingredients. That's why we've cultivated relationships with local farmers, fishermen, and artisan producers who share our commitment to quality.

Our menu changes with the seasons, celebrating the natural rhythm of the earth and ensuring that every ingredient is at its peak. This philosophy extends to our wine cellar, where we curate selections that complement our cuisine perfectly.
