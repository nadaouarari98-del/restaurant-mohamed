Kunafa, Queen of Arabic Sweets

Crown your events with our Kunafa, the long beloved Queen of Arabic Sweets, freshly crafted by Fayrooz Gourmet, the only premium confectionery and bakehouse in Sharjah’s Al Zahia neighborhood.

Book our Kunafa Cart as your very own Kunafa Concierge.

Standard inclusions:

  • 4 hours live station plus 1 hour ingress and 1 hour egress
  • Minimum of 100 individual servings
  • One serving attendant
  • Transportation to & from in-scope locations

Upgrades available: Coffee and/or tea service, expanded dessert selection, additional hours, extra servings, and supplementary staffing.

Chargeable: Transportation to & from out-of-scope locations.

Using time-honored techniques and made with the best quality ingredients, Fayrooz Kunafa is a standout dessert sure to delight your guests and make your gatherings so much sweeter, more special, and incredibly memorable.
